# Mammalian Phylogeny

This dataset contains a 20-taxon phylogenetic tree of representative mammals spanning major placental and non-placental lineages.

## Source & References

Topology based on:
- Murphy et al. 2001 (Nature 409:614-618)
- Meredith et al. 2011 (Science 334:521-524)

Branch lengths represent approximate divergence times in units of 0.1 hundred million years (MYA).

## Included Taxa

Primates: Human, Chimpanzee, Gorilla, Orangutan, Macaque
Rodentia: Mouse, Rat
Lagomorpha: Rabbit
Carnivora: Dog, Cat
Perissodactyla: Horse
Artiodactyla: Cow, Pig, Sheep, Whale
Chiroptera: Bat
Proboscidea: Elephant
Cingulata: Armadillo
Didelphimorphia: Opossum
Monotremata: Platypus
